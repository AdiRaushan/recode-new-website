Frequently Asked Questions

Everything You Might Want To Know

Quick answers about our process, timelines, pricing models, and collaboration.

What's included in your website packages?
Our website packages include everything your business needs to get online: custom design tailored to your brand, mobile-responsive layout, basic SEO optimization, contact forms, social media integration, and hosting for the first year. We also provide a content management system so you can easily update your site, plus basic training on how to use it. Additional features like e-commerce functionality, blog setup, and advanced analytics can be added based on your specific needs.
How long does it take to build my website?
Most small to medium business websites are completed within 2-4 weeks from the time we receive all your content and requirements. The timeline depends on the complexity of your site and how quickly you provide feedback during the design process. Simple brochure websites typically take 2 weeks, while more complex sites with custom features or extensive product catalogs may take 4-6 weeks. We'll give you a specific timeline during your initial consult
Do you provide ongoing support after my website launches?
Yes, we offer comprehensive post-launch support to ensure your website continues running smoothly. This includes technical maintenance, security updates, backup services, and minor content updates. We offer different support packages ranging from basic maintenance to full-service management where we handle all updates and improvements for you. You'll never be left wondering how to fix a technical issue or make important updates to your site.
Can I update my website content myself?
Absolutely! We build all our websites with user-friendly content management systems that allow you to easily update text, images, prices, and other content without any technical knowledge. We provide training and documentation to show you how to make these updates. For more complex changes or if you prefer not to handle updates yourself, our team is always available to make changes for you at affordable rates.
